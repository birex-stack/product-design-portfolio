Elastic Cases

Connecting scattered signals into shared cases.

A native case management experience across Elastic Security and Observability that helps analysts and incident responders connect alerts, events and notes into one investigation — without rebuilding context from scratch.

My role: Design lead

The Problem

Analysts detected anomalies across logs, metrics and alerts — but turning isolated signals into coherent investigations was manual and inconsistent. Cases lived in spreadsheets, chat threads and ticket systems. Teams needed a native workflow inside Elastic that fit how they already worked.

Key Insights

  • Cases succeed when they reduce context-switching — not when they add process on top of existing tools.
  • Investigations are memory problems before they're data problems — similar past cases were the most valued and hardest to find.
  • A case is a container for context — not a ticket with a title and a status field.
  • Adoption followed when Cases lived where analysts already worked, not in a separate toolchain.
  • Analysts expect the AI chat to support the full workflow — investigate, manage cases, and act — not hand off back to the UI after every step.

Solution

Investigate with full context in one place.

Case detail brings activity, attachments and similar cases together — with severity, assignees and classification always visible while analysts work through the incident.

Investigate and manage cases in AI chat.

With Elastic AI Agent, analysts can run a full investigation in conversation — correlate linked cases, summarize findings, escalate to IR, and update case status without leaving chat.

Prioritize what needs attention now.

The cases overview surfaces open workload, status and severity at a glance — so teams filter, assign and jump into the right investigation without losing momentum.

Adapt cases to how your team works.

A template editor pairs schema definition with a live fields preview — teams define fields, values and conditions in any structure their workflow needs, without leaving the product.

User outcomes / impact

  • Less context-switching Pilot teams reduced tool-switching during investigations by keeping signals and notes in one workflow.
  • Full workflow in AI chat Elastic AI Agent supports end-to-end investigation and case management in conversation — triage, correlate, escalate, and update status without returning to the traditional UI.
  • Faster case creation Playback sessions showed analysts creating cases from alerts more quickly than with external tools.
  • Enterprise adoption Adopted by security teams within the Elastic customer base as their primary investigation hub.
  • Unified investigation model Established a shared case lifecycle across Elastic Security and Observability — one investigation model for security teams and beyond, aligned for engineering and design.
