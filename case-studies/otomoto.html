Marketplace · Otomoto

Helping people make better decisions when choosing a vehicle.

A mobile-first discovery experience on Poland's largest vehicle marketplace that helps everyday car buyers find and compare the right listings — without filter fatigue.

My role: Design lead

The Problem

Millions of listings, but buyers struggled to find vehicles that matched their real needs — not just their search filters. Discovery was filter-driven, not intent-driven. Mobile users abandoned when refining searches felt like work, and comparison happened outside the product entirely.

Key Insights

Conversion follows confidence — buyers need to trust results, not see more results.

Buyers don't want more filters — they want fewer wrong listings in their shortlist.

Buyers think in use cases ("family car", "city commute") — not spec sheets or exhaustive filter panels.

Discovery is a decision funnel, not a search engine — less searching, more deciding.

Solution

Discovery built around intent — from homepage entry through listing detail, so buyers compare with confidence instead of fighting filters.

User outcomes / impact

Improved conversion A/B tests showed better shortlist-to-contact conversion with relevance-first discovery.
More engaged mobile sessions Sessions grew longer with fewer filter interactions — buyers spent time deciding, not configuring search.
Scale Rolled out to millions of monthly active buyers across Poland's largest vehicle marketplace.
"Found it faster" User feedback consistently highlighted faster discovery and higher confidence in shortlisted vehicles.
